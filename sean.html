My favorite pasttimes:

My name is Sean Bass and I am a Sophomore and San Diego State University.
My favorite things to do in my free time are snowboarding and basketball.

Since I began to snowboard I have always felt a certain calling to be on the mountain. There is nothing more exhilarating than jumping and carving down a steep mountain in the beatiful fresh air.

Below is a rough schedule of what I have to do before hitting the mountain:
  1. Wake up early and take a shower
  2. Wax my board and sharpen my edges
  3. Pack my snowbag including making my lunch and snacks
  4. Get all my gear together
  5. Drive up the mountain towards Tahoe
  6. Get to the mountain and begin my day
